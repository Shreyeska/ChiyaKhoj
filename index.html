Locations of Top 5 places to drink Tea

Chiyawala - Bakhundol Branch

Location: Lalitpur

Chiyawala, nestled in a charming neighborhood, exudes a homely and cozy ambiance. The inviting space is adorned with earthy tones, plush cushions, and warm lighting, creating a haven for relaxation. The gentle aroma of freshly brewed teas, friendly staff, and rustic furnishings contribute to a comforting atmosphere. Guests can choose from a curated selection of teas served in charming teapots, accompanied by handmade pastries. Communal tables and cozy nooks foster a sense of community, inviting patrons to unwind, savor the moment, and escape the outside world. It's a cherished home away from home, where every detail ensures a warm and inviting experience.

Chiya Spot - Boudha Branch

Location: Kathmandu

Welcome to our tea haven, a place where tea is an art and aesthetics take center stage. Our tea shop is a visual delight, harmonizing modern elegance with timeless charm. Every detail, from the minimalist decor to the carefully chosen teaware, contributes to a symphony of aesthetic pleasure. Teas are presented as a gallery of colors and aromas, served in exquisite, handcrafted teacups and teapots that elevate the experience. Soft ambient lighting and curated artworks create a tranquil atmosphere, inviting patrons to immerse themselves in a sensory journey. In this tea sanctuary, the pursuit of beauty is as integral as the perfect infusion, crafting a unique and visually enchanting tea experience.
